Description:


Click and drag to create shapes. Top Right holds combo boxes for shape and colour selection, and slider for line thickness.

Ctrl-Z to undo, Ctrl-S to save. Clear button and File->Clear both remove all elements.


Shapes:

  • Rectangle
  • Oval
  • Line
  • Arc

Colours:

  • Red
  • Green
  • Blue
  • Yellow
  • Cyan
  • Grey
